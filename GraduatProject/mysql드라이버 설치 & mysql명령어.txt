(2)압축 푼다: tar zxvf mysql-connector-java-5.1.26.tar.gz
(3)jar을 적절한 곳에 옮긴다:
$JAVA_HOME/jre/lib/ext
(4)환경변수 추가
vi /etc/profile
export CLASSPATH=.:$JAVA_HOME/lib/mysql-connector-java-5.1.26-bin.jar
(5)변경사항 적용
sh /etc/profile
